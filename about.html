Our Story

Rooted in Ritual

Grind House opened in 2016 with a simple idea: coffee should be unhurried. We source directly from small family farms, roast in-house every week, and pour every cup like it's the only one that matters.

This is a place to slow down — worn wood tables, morning light, and the quiet hum of the grinder. Pull up a chair and stay a while.

01

Quality

Beans sourced direct from small family farms and roasted in-house every week, never more than a few days off the roaster.

02

Community

A neighborhood living room first, a coffee shop second — regulars, first dates, and quiet mornings all welcome.

03

Craft

Every cup pulled with care, from grind size to pour speed. Nothing rushed, nothing automated.
